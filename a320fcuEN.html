A320 Flight Control Unit Replica

This project is in 3 parts :
- Electronics (electronic circuit for multiplexing and printed circuit board design)
- Mechanical design(rotary encoder with push/pull function)
- Software (interface between hardware, computer and simulator)

Electronics

1

Multiplexing inputs and outputs

This modules contains 9 push buttons, 19 leds and 16 7-segment digit. 7-segment displays are controlled with 2 MAX7219 drivers, inputs and ouputs are controlled via shift registers. Leds for blacklight are also included. A connector regroup all the signals to be passed to a microcontroller.
