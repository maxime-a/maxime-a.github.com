Korry Button Replica

A design of Korry like button to be easily mounted on panels for flight simulation.

Korry replica V1.1

To create the engraved front plate I poured epoxy resin in the front plate, painted it and engraved it with laser. The body is paint with metallic paint for more realism. Below you can see a backlight test (color of light is just for test purpose, it is not corresponding to real). Korry is also mounted on a 3D printed plate only for test purpose.
